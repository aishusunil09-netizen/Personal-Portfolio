Hello there

I'm Aishwarya —
a data scientist in the making

Growing up, I was always the person asking "why?" about everything — why do patterns repeat, why do people behave the way they do, why does one small change ripple outward into big effects? Data science turned out to be the field where all those questions finally had a home.

I'm currently pursuing my studies with a focus on data science, machine learning, and statistical analysis. I believe data is only meaningful when it's communicated well — a beautiful model that nobody understands is just a beautiful model. I want to build bridges between numbers and people.

When I'm not wrangling datasets, you'll find me with a good book, trying out a new recipe, or planning my next adventure somewhere with a mountain view and good coffee.
